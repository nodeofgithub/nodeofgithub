Progress Update 8 (July 4, 2021)


Changed enemy death animation to stumble backwards, then switch to ragdoll physics mid-animation.
Headshot skips death animation and immediately puts enemy in ragdoll physics.
Enemy drops rifle as a pickup object which adds ammo to player.

Progress Update 7 (July 4, 2021)


Strange bugged behavior while attempting to implement enemy ragdoll physics.
The problem was that the ragdoll physics, animation system and AI system were all fighting over control.
The solution was just to make sure ragdoll physics are inactive while enemy is alive by setting all the rigidbodies to kinematic in script.
